ABOUT ANALISIS

Analisis, the AI-powered application made to aid you anywhere anytime .

Our team of students has developed a solution that detects wounds using AI

and provides you with a step-by-step guide on how to treat the injury.

Whether you're dealing with a minor scratch or a more serious injury, Analisis will be there.

We are working on improving Analisis to provide our users with the best experience possible.
